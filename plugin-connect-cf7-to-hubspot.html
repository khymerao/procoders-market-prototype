What Connect CF7 to HubSpot Does

Connect CF7 to HubSpot by ProCoders is a powerful plugin that connects WordPress Contact Form 7 directly with your HubSpot account, automating your workflow and enhancing lead management. With this plugin, every submission from Contact Form 7 is instantly and securely synced with HubSpot, allowing you to manage contacts, track submissions, and engage with your audience without manual data entry. Third-Party Service Integration: This plugin integrates with HubSpot, a third-party service, to manage and sync data from Contact Form 7 submissions. By using this plugin, data collected through Contact Form 7 will be sent to HubSpot for processing and storage. Features

  • Automated syncing of Contact Form 7 submissions to HubSpot Contacts, Deals, and Lead Capture Forms
  • Intuitive field mapping to HubSpot properties, including custom fields
  • Latest HubSpot API for security and reliability
  • Optional async mode — visitors submit instantly while HubSpot syncs in the background via Action Scheduler or WP-Cron
  • Automatic retries with exponential backoff (up to three attempts) for failed jobs
  • GDPR/consent handling and resubscribe UX for blocked submissions
  • Journey behavioral events: cf7_form_submitted, cf7_form_synced, cf7_form_sync_failed

Installation

  1. Download the plugin from WordPress.org (or ProCoders Market for Pro) and unzip the archive.
  2. Upload the connect-cf7-to-hubspot folder to your /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
  3. Activate the plugin through the Plugins menu in WordPress.
  4. Navigate to the plugin settings page and follow the on-screen instructions to connect your HubSpot private app token.

WordPress Multisite

Upload and install via Network Admin → Plugins → Add New. Do not network-activate — activate on a per-site basis for precise control.
